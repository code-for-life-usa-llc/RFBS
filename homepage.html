Struggles for immigrants when finding work or jobs

Most Immigrants come to the U.S because they want to get a better life and find jobs to provide for their family. In the process of that their can be some consequences. Such as English proficiency, and lack of advanced degrees make it hard for some immigrants to find high-skilled jobs in the U.S.

5 Facts About Immigration

  • Most immigrants come to the U.S. because of employment opportunities, to escape a violent conflict, environmental factors, educational purposes, or to reunite with family
  • Most immigrants come from Mexico but not all they come from all over the world.
  • Immigration can be important because immigrants are more likely to be working age, they help fill gaps in the workforce, particularly in certain critical industries
  • Most immigrants come to the U.S becuase it has better work but not all.
